Data

Your data is by no means sold to other companies. We collect your data when you accept the terms of service when signing up. The data is yours and when you delete your account, we do not keep anything.

When you read a blog post, no matter if you have an account or not, a read counter will update the amount of reads on that page. It only adds to the count when you open a post in full view and counting is done by collecting your IP in a session. Once the session has concluded your IP will be removed Users with accounts will use there user name when reading to count towards the total.
